About us

2017

In the vibrant city of Astana, we had a vision to create a haven where beauty meets expertise. In 2017, armed with a passion for cosmetology and a determination to make a mark in the industry, We opened the doors to RadiantElegance.

2018

Over the next few years, RadiantElegance faced its share of challenges and triumphs. The team expanded, bringing together a talented group of professionals dedicated to the art of cosmetology. As word spread about the exceptional service and personalized touch offered by RadiantElegance, the client base grew, and the business flourished.
